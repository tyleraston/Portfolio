# Overview

The projects I create serve as valuable tools in my professional role as a Developmental Technician, enabling me to better focus on assisting adults with developmental disabilities in their growth and achievement of developmental goals. The demands of my environment require constant attention, and the efficiency of my workflow is crucial. To address this, I have developed a tool that enhances my ability to access and manage documentation for each individual more effectively, allowing me to dedicate more time and attention to their unique needs and developmental progress.

My primary responsibility involves working within groups of 8-12 individuals, each of whom has an extensive binder containing their pertinent documentation. Managing this documentation often necessitates multiple trips to storage cabinets, diverting my attention away from the group. This application addresses this challenge by providing me with convenient access to their documentation on a tablet. The information is organized in a list format, with each person's documentation stored under their name in a collapsible list item, streamlining the process and allowing me to focus more on the individuals in the group.

While the application is still in its early stages and requires further development, it serves as a foundational starting point for a larger project. This undertaking will not only enable me to contribute to a more substantial and useful project in the future but also serve as a valuable opportunity for learning mobile application development. The hands-on experience gained through this process will further enhance my skills and understanding of the intricacies involved in creating impactful mobile applications.

# Development Environment

The initial stage of this project was developed entirely using Android Studio. While initially considering React Native, further research led me to pivot towards Kotlin with a brief contemplation of Native C++. The ultimate decision was influenced by the project's time constraints, prompting a focus on the efficient development capabilities offered by using Kotlin/Android SDK within the given timeframe.
